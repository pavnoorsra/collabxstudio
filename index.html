What is CollabX Studio?

A collaboration hub for singers and producers. Singers share vocals, producers share music or beats, and artists connect to start new projects together.

Why this exists

Social media is noisy and serious collaborations are hard to find. CollabX Studio is built only for music collaboration, without distractions.

How it works

1. Create a profile

Add your role, genre, and upload your MP3 vocals or music.

2. Discover artists

Browse singers and producers and listen to their work.

3. Start collaborating

Connect and work together on new music projects.
